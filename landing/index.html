Designed for Guyana

Offline Intelligence, Heritage Preservation

Built with lightweight on-device technologies to run translations smoothly in remote areas, with zero mobile data consumption.

On-Device AI Engine

Powered by Google Gemma models compiled directly for mobile hardware. Enjoy smart, context-aware translations that understand grammar structure offline.

Offline Speech-to-Text

Speak into the microphone. Acoustic calculations are performed locally on your device using ONNX runtimes. Instantly transcribes spoken English or Spanish without server latency.

Guyanese Creolese Core

Bridging the gap for Guyana's cultural dialect. Carefully mapped translation logic translates formal English and Spanish to authentic Creolese idioms and phrases.

Absolute Data Privacy

No cloud database. No voice logs stored in corporate servers. Your history and translation phrasebooks are saved exclusively in your device's secure local sandbox.
